We are The Colorizer

The Colorizer is a website covering worldly affairs in a multicultural sense. Ranging from international diplomacy to pop culture, both big talks and small talks, we promote intercultural discussions of all sides and topics to improve goodwill and understanding in this globalized world. We aim to be a roundtable of diverse ideas and viewpoints. Informative, educational, and thought-provoking. With The Colorizer, you can color your horizon.

Glocalizing your life

We deliver local stories to the world, and overseas happenings to your room. We showcase small but meaningful tidings of here and there around the world. We are also looking for minority opinions, alternative solutions, and unheard thoughts beyond fact itself, that we can share and broaden the conversation. Even though The Colorizer is not the press, we seek high journalistic quality and standards when it comes to news reports.

Focus on human stories

One of our primary interests is people, someone like you, someone like us. Because human matters. We are always open and ready to listen to your stories. We want to ask questions to get to know more about you and your world. Everyone's voice is worth sharing. Color us with your own stories.

In the spirit of inclusive diversity

The Colorizer appreciates unique beauty and value of every culture and identity on earth. We also spotlight lesser-known cultural colors that deserve fair attention. If you turn your unfamiliarity and fear into curiosity, a colorful parade of cultures appears. Feel free to spectate, it's even better if you can join with us.

In the end, solidarity

The Colorizer is an advocate of United Nations Universal Declaration of Human Rights and following humanitarian causes. Beyond borders, beyond cultures, everyone should be respected equally without discrimination and oppression. Despite disharmony and conflict we might have, despite aggression and hostility we might face, we have a faith in collective power weaved in compassion and empathy. Instead of fueling feud and hate, let's find out how to solve and prevent problems. Let's talk about what needs to be done and what we can do together with a strong sense of realism.

"The Colorizer is a small but ambitious effort to help people of different backgrounds bond together. A colorful mosaic made of goodness; this is how this idea was born. This is also my personal journey to discover unfamiliar worlds and break my own prejudices as well. The Colorizer is not a lecturing authority but a classroom of students that we can learn where our differences come from and respect it as-is."

— Junsub Kim, Editor-in-chief of The Colorizer
